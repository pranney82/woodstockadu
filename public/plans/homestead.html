The largest single-level plan — over a thousand square feet of comfortable, step-free living with a two-car garage. The Homestead is built for the long haul: a place you could genuinely move into for years, not just a guest suite.

Who it’s for

  • Couples downsizing into the ADU and renting out the main house
  • A high-value long-term rental with full-size living space
  • Families who want generous single-level living plus a garage

The layout

A spacious one-floor layout with a full-size living and kitchen area, a comfortable bedroom and bath, and a two-car garage — all step-free. At 1,032 sq ft it lives like a small house, which is why it carries the lowest price per square foot of the five plans even though the total is higher.

Common questions

Why does the Homestead need a 2,580 sq ft main home?
Because an ADU larger than 850 sq ft is capped at 40% of the primary home’s floor area, the two largest plans require larger main homes. Our tool checks your home’s size automatically. See the size rule →
Is a bigger plan a better deal?
Per square foot, yes — the Homestead is about $258/sq ft versus ~$300 for the smallest plans, because fixed costs spread over more space. Compare all five →
Will The Homestead fit on my lot?
It needs roughly a 2,580 sq ft main home and a code-compliant spot behind or beside it. Our address tool checks both against the City’s data in about a minute. Check your lot →
