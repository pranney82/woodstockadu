How to choose

  • Need step-free living? The single-level Cottage (no garage) and Bungalow (with garage) are the most accessible.
  • Tight on yard space? The Carriage and Overlook stack living space over the garage, so they need the least ground area.
  • Want the most home? The Homestead and Overlook are the largest — and cost the least per square foot.
  • Remember the size rule: the three compact plans (631–684 sq ft) fit any size main home, thanks to the City’s 850 sq ft allowance. The larger Homestead and Overlook can be up to 40% of your main home’s floor area, so they need roughly 2,580 and 2,660 sq ft primary homes. See the rules →
