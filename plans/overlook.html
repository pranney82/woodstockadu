The flagship. The Overlook is the largest of the five — a two-story living loft above a two-car garage, finished with a private second-floor deck. It’s the plan for homeowners who want a little separation, a view, and outdoor living to go with it.

Who it’s for

  • A standout guest house or premium rental that commands top rent
  • Homeowners who want elevated, private living with a deck
  • Anyone maximizing square footage and resale appeal on the lot

The layout

A two-car garage anchors the ground floor; upstairs is a full one-bedroom home — open living and kitchen, bedroom, full bath — opening onto a private deck. At 1,064 sq ft it’s the most square footage of any plan, and the raised living level gives it light and privacy the single-level plans can’t match. It’s the home pictured on our front page for a reason.
