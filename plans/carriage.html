The smallest footprint of the five — and the smartest use of a tight lot. The Carriage builds up instead of out: a complete one-bedroom home perched over a two-car garage, so you keep your yard and gain covered parking, a workshop, or storage underneath.

Who it’s for

  • Homeowners on narrow or shallow lots who can’t give up ground-floor yard space
  • A private, separate space for an adult child or a home office, with a garage below
  • Anyone who wants parking and storage built into the ADU footprint

The layout

Downstairs is a full two-car garage. Upstairs, a stair leads to an open living-kitchen, a bedroom, and a full bath — a genuine standalone home, not a bonus room. Because it stacks, the Carriage needs the least open ground area of any plan, which makes it the go-to for lots where the Cottage or Bungalow won’t fit behind the house.
